Fresh & Fantastical Flavours

At POTiON, we only use fresh organic herbs and berries sustainably harvested from our enchanted forests. This gives our brews their amazing flavours and fantastical effects. We have 3 flavours - Vita Berry, Wild Berry, and Super Berry. Try them all.

Enchanted Energy

POTiON is 100% enchanted. Just one drink can power you up through an entire gaming session. On your 100th battleroyale match for the night? No sweat, you’ve got enchanted energy running through your veins.

Locally Brewed

No outsourcing here, we only use local cauldrons. So you know when you buy POTiON you get the best brew money can buy and support local witches in the process. Now that’s an achievment!
